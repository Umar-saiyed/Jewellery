jewelry Care

Keep your precious pieces looking as stunning as the day you got them with these simple care tips:

  • Store your jewelry in a dry, clean place.
  • Avoid contact with water, perfumes, or harsh chemicals.
  • Clean your jewelry with a soft cloth after each wear.
